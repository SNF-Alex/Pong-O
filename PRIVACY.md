# Privacy Policy for Pong-O

**Last Updated: November 3, 2025**

## Overview
Pong-O ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

### Sign In with Apple (Optional)
- When you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier to save your game progress across devices
- No email address or personal information is collected or stored by us
- Apple handles all authentication securely

### Game Data (Local Storage)
- Game progress (coins, unlocked items, high scores)
- Game settings (control preferences, selected skins/themes)
- All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage

### Cloud Sync (Optional)
- If you sign in with Apple, your game progress may sync via iCloud
- This is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure
- We do not have access to your iCloud data

## Information We Don't Collect
- We do NOT collect personal information
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking
- We do NOT display advertisements
- We do NOT share your data with third parties
- We do NOT use cookies

## Data Usage
- Game data is used solely to provide game functionality
- Your progress is saved to enhance your gaming experience
- No data is transmitted to external servers (except Apple's iCloud if you enable Sign In)

## Children's Privacy
Our app is rated 12+ and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Your Rights
- You can delete all local game data by uninstalling the app
- You can sign out of Apple Sign In at any time in the app settings
- Your data will be deleted when you delete the app

## Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, contact: aep2cool@icloud.com

## No Sale of Data
We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to outside parties.

## Security
Game data is stored securely on your device. Cloud sync uses Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure.
